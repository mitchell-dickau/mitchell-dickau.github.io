I am a climate scientist working as a post-doctoral fellow at Concordia University in Montréal, Quebec. In my research, I use climate models to investigate questions related to the climate outcomes of overshoot, remaining carbon budgets, the impact of temporary carbon storage, and the effect of climate change on outdoor skating in Montréal.
